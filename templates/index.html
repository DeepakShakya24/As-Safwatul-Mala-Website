As Safwatul Mala

Lines taken from Muqaddimmah By Imam Ibn ul Jazari

The following lines taken from Muqaddimmah, very aptly define Tajweed and its importance. In a very beautiful poetry Imam Jazari throws light upon the subject, thus encouraging us to learn Tajweed

What is Tajweed

Translation

It(Tajweed) is also the beautificationof recitation,And anadornmentof pronunciationand reading.

Translation

And it(Tajweed) is giving the letterits required rightsOf each and every characteristic as well as (giving each letter its) presentedrights (or dues)

The above lines clearly state that Tajweed is the art of reading the Book of Allah correctly. Learning the rules of recitation and reading them beautifully is an onus on every Muslim.

Importance of Tajweed

Translation

The practical application of Tajweed is without doubt compulsory. Who does not read the Quran correctlyis a sinner

Translation

Because this Quran was revealed to us by Allah in this formAnd exactly like this(with Tajweed)it reached us (from our Prophet, then related in authentic chains over generations to our present day Shuyookh and those with ijaazah)

The above lines throw light upon the importance of Tajweed in the life of a Muslim. Itis deemed compulsory on us to learn Tajweed so that we can read the Book of Allah as it was revealed.

About As Safwatul Mala

As-Safwatul Mala

Among the High-Ranking Elite

The word is taken from the PoetryHirz Al Amani Wa Wajhat Tahani By Imam Shatabi

In the prelude to understanding the word, its important we refer to various ahadith highlighting the precious place a Qariof Quran has in the eyes of Allah.

The following words explain the word As-Safwatul mala and the importance of a reciter of Quran

So, O reciter of the Quran! Hold firmly on to it, honoring and respecting it in all conditions

In this line the Qari is addressed regarding the qualities that he should have. In the following line the rewardsfor the Qari are given.

And those who hold fast to the Book (act on its teachings) and perform salah. We will never waste the rewards of those who do righteous deeds.

In a Hadith, itcomes

The Book of Allah, in it is guidance and nur, so grab on to the Book of Allah and hold firmly on to it.

In another Hadith it is reported:

Whoever holds on to it,(The Quran) and practices upon it will be upon guidance, and whoever fails to do so will be misled.

It means someone who honorsand exalts will have the same meaning

Enjoy well (all comingyour way in the gardens of paradise!)
Your parents will be adorned with garments of pure light, crown (of honor) and (other)adornments.

Explanation

So what do you think of the children (Qari-of these parents) when they are rewarded? They are the family of Allah and the high-ranking elite

Explanation

Verily Allah has family from amongst man. It was said: who are they O Messenger of Allah? He saw replied: The people of the Quran, they are the family of Allah and his close and dear ones.”

The above verses and hadith explain vehemently the meaning and origin of the word As Safwatul Mala.-Among the high Ranking Elite. The ranks of the people whom Allah loves and calls them His Family. Thepeople who are beloved to Him. Aperson who recites the Quran in the way it was revealed will be amongst them.
