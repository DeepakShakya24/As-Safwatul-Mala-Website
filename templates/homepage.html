As Safwatul Mala

This platform is a humble effort for teaching Tajweed to Sisters. Spearheaded by a very talented and gifted teacher, whom Allah chose for this cause. The explicit purpose of forming this platform is spreading the correct way of reciting the Book of Allah. Spreading the knowledge related to the Tajweed, the science of reciting Quran correctly. We pray and hope that Tajweedis made widespread and reaches every household. With aspirations of being the family of Allah, we pray and hope the purpose of this platform is met by adding more and more people to the High-Ranking Elite.
